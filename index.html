About Us

Welcome to The Godfather, Silchar's premier destination for authentic Indian cuisine. Located at Hotel Swagat on Central Road, we bring you the rich flavors and aromatic spices of traditional Indian cooking.


Our chefs craft each dish with passion, using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients. Whether you are craving classic North Indian curries, aromatic biryanis, or regional specialties, we promise an unforgettable dining experience.
