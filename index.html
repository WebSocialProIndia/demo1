Our Story

Upper River Restaurant is an unfussy eatery nestled in the heart of York, serving authentic homemade siu mei dishes and traditional Asian staples since day one.

Our recipes are crafted with care, using time-honored techniques passed down through generations. Every dim sum is handmade fresh daily, and our stir-fried noodles are cooked to perfection with the finest ingredients.
